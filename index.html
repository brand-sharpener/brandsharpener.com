What We Do

The room where marketers figure out AI together

Brand Sharpener is a practitioner-led community, not a webinar series. Every month, working marketers bring real tools to the table, run live demos, and have honest conversations about what's actually working. No vendor pitches. No hype. Just sharp people making each other sharper.

Our Members

Marketing and AI leaders learning in real time

Our members span marketing, strategy, data, and creative disciplines, from CMOs and creative directors to data strategists and founders. We're practitioners and leaders who share what works and what doesn't to accelerate progress together.

How It Works

Who We Are

Not for spectators

Brand Sharpener is a growing community of active AI enthusiasts, marketers and makers who are testing, building, and learning in real time. You don't need to be an AI expert. You just need to be curious, collaborative, and willing to share what you're trying, whether it's a success or a work-in-progress.
